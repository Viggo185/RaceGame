# Racing Game

This game is a top-down 2D racing game focused on local multiplayer competition. Players race against each other on the same track, navigating turns, avoiding obstacles, and managing speed across different surfaces. The goal is to complete laps as fast as possible and achieve the best lap times.

Key Features
* ### Movement:

Players control cars with smooth and responsive driving mechanics, including acceleration, braking, and turning.

* ### Track System:

The track is built from modular pieces, allowing for custom layouts with curves, straights, and obstacles.

* ### Surface Physics:

Different surfaces affect speed and handling:

Road → fast driving
Sand → slows the car
* ### Collision System:

Cars can collide with barriers and obstacles, affecting movement and triggering crash effects.

* ### Crash & Respawn System:

After multiple crashes, players respawn at the last checkpoint, maintaining race flow.

* ### Checkpoint System:

Players must pass checkpoints in order to ensure they stay on track and cannot skip sections.

* ### Lap System:

Tracks completed laps and ensures correct race progression.

* ### Lap Timing:

Measures lap times and sector times (time between checkpoints) for each player.

* ### Best Lap Tracking:

Records the fastest lap time for each player and compares results at the end of the race.

* ### UI System:

Displays:

Current lap
Lap time
Sector times
Race results
* ### Finish Screen:

Shows the winner and best lap times after the race ends, with an option to restart.

* ### Visual Effects:

Includes animated explosion effects when crashing, adding feedback and polish.

* ### Local Multiplayer:

Two players can race on the same device using different controls (e.g., WASD and arrow keys).

* ### Goal

Compete against other players, master the track, and set the fastest lap time to win the race
